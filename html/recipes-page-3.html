Blue cheese, pear and chicken salad

Ingredients:

  • chicken breast - 1pc.
  • danablu cheese - 100 g
  • romaine lettuce - 70 g
  • red leaf lettuce - 70 g
  • arugula - 50 g
  • walnut - 70 g
  • sunflower seeds - 50 g

For dressing:

  • maple syrup - 2 tbsp

Fry chicken breast in a pan until it cooked. Slice the pear. Cut the cheese into small cubes. Grind the nuts. Mix all the ingredients together, sprinkle it with maple syrup. Add seeds on top.
