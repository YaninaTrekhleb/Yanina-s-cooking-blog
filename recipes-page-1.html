Salad with baked eggplant, red pepper and feta cheese

Ingredients: - eggplant - 1pc. - red pepper - 1 pc - feta cheese - 50 g - cherry tomatoes - 5 pc - cucumber - 1 pc - red onion - 1pc - lettuce leaves - 100 g For dressing: - olive oil - 2 tbsp - balsamic vinegar - 1 tbsp Eggplant and red pepper bake in the oven (for 25-35 minutes), cut into strips. Cut the onion, feta cheese, cherry tomato, cucumber. Mix all ingredients together and add the lettuce leaves , dress with vinegar and olive oil. Salt and pepper to taste.
