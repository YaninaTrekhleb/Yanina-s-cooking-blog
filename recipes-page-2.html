Graipefruit shrimp salad

Ingredients:

  • shrimps - 300 g
  • broccoli - 150 g
  • grapefruit - 1 pc
  • arugula - 70 g
  • sunflower seeds

For dressing:

  • honey - 2 tbsp
  • lemon juice - 1 tbsp
  • mustard - 1tbsp

Boil the shrimp and broccoli until it cooked. Peel the grapefruit and cut into slices. Put all the ingredients in a bowl. Mix honey, mustard and lemon juice. Dress the salad with dressing, sprinkle with sunflower seeds.
